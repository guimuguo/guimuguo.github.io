Biography

I am a tenure-track assistant professor of the Department of Computer Sciences at Rowan University. I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from the University of Alabama at Birmingham. Before that, I received my Master degree from Tongji University, Shanghai. My current research interests are Parallel and distributed computing, Graph Mining, Machine Learning and Deep learning.

Ph.D. Positions: I am actively hiring Ph.D. students to join my group as research assistants. All the positions will be fully funded. Please feel free to contact me, and please include your CV and transcripts.
